BMO Bank on 222 Main Street

     In 1910 a bank was build on 222 Main Street. However this building was used as by BMO bank for only twenty years. From 1931 till 1933 the building was used by the Scandinavian United Church. Afterwards the Army & Navy Veterans in Canada took over the building and used if during the Second World War. In 1947 the was ocupied by photographers and Steffens-Colmer Ltd. Trans-Canada Films joined them in 1950. By 1952 the building was empty.
